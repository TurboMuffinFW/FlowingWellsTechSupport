How To Find Your Computer IP Address

at the bottom right corner of your screen, where your clock lives, there are a couple icons. On the left edge of the box, there are two upward facing arrows. Click those arrows to display more icons. In the box of extra icons, there is a white square icon with a big black "V" in the center of it. Hover your mouse above it, don't actually click it, and your IP address will be displayed. It will look something like "10.11.X.X"
